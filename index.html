Screening for Shariah-Compliance

In order to screen for Shariah-Compliance, Gold Igloo implements a rigid screening process that involves 3 screening stages: Business activities, financial debt and finally dividend purification.

Business Activity

Is this company involved in a prohibited activity?

Prohibited activities include tobacco, entertainment, alcohol, financial services and gambling.
Financial Screening Activity

Does this company have more than 33% debt over it's total assets

Companies should not exceed 33.33% financial ratio in total debt over total assets.
